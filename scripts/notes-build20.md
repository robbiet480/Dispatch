Build 20 (version 1.0)

NEW: APPLE WATCH APP
- Dispatch is on your wrist. Answer prompts from forwarded notifications (Yes/No file straight from the notification), file quick reports with on-watch sensors (location, weather, heart rate, battery), and see your streak on complications. Reports note which device filed them.

NEW: IPAD
- Proper iPad layout: reports sidebar + detail split view, two-column visualizations and insights, readable-width forms, ⌘N for a new report, ⌘F to search.

IMPROVED
- Yes/no questions auto-advance to the next question when you answer.
- Survey Cancel/Next buttons and progress bar no longer wash out against themes.
- You can swipe between questions during a report (always could — now it's tested).

2. Answer a prompt's Yes/No directly from the watch notification.
3. On iPad: browse reports in the split view, file a report, try ⌘N/⌘F with a keyboard.
