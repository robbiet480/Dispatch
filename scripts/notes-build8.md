Build 8 — the big one. What's new:

WIDGETS & CONTROL CENTER
- Home Screen + Lock Screen widgets: time since your last report, today's count, streak, and next prompt.
- Control Center button: one tap starts a new report from anywhere.

WEEKLY DIGEST
- New Weekly Digest screen: your week summarized on-device by Apple Intelligence (report counts, top activities/people/places, mood trend, steps & workouts). Optional Sunday-evening digest notification (off by default, in Notification settings).

NEW SENSORS
- Medications: doses you logged in Health now attach to reports. You'll get one new Health permission prompt on first launch.
- Staircases now count DOWN as well as up (new Motion permission prompt).
- Activity Rings captured with every report (from build 7, now visible in detail).

SYNC
- iCloud sync now pushes in the background — reports from your other device appear without opening the app.

UNDER THE HOOD
- Your data store moves into a shared container on first launch (enables widgets). Everything should carry over — if anything looks missing, check iCloud sync status in Settings > Data and tell Robbie immediately.

WHAT TO TEST
1. First launch after updating: expect two new permission prompts (Motion, Medications). Report should feel identical afterward.
2. Add the widget + try the Control Center button.
3. Open Weekly Digest (Settings) — does the narrative feel accurate to your week?
4. File a report; confirm the widget updates.
5. If you work out: finish a workout with a workout-end prompt group configured and confirm the prompt includes the workout details.
