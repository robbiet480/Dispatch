NEW: NUMBER INPUT STYLES
- Number questions can now use a slider, stepper, rotary dial, tap counter, or rating scale instead of the keyboard — pick per question in the editor (INPUT STYLE). Try the dial. We are proud of the dial.

NEW: ICLOUD DRIVE BACKUPS
- Backups now also sync to iCloud Drive (Files > Dispatch > Backups on any device, incl. your Mac). Settings > Data picks the destination; local stays the guaranteed copy. Note: the iCloud Drive folder can take a while (or a reboot) to first appear — Apple caches folder visibility.
