Build 15 — Insights

NEW: INSIGHTS
- Settings > Insights (also linked from the Weekly Digest): on-device pattern analysis across all your reports. Things like "Reports where you mention gym average 6,000 more steps" or "You answer Yes to Working on 85% of reports at Office."
- Built to be honest: patterns only appear with enough data behind them (10+ reports on each side), meaningful differences only, capped at 8, and it will tell you plainly when nothing is strong enough to show.
- The Weekly Digest now weaves your top insights into its summary.

WHAT TO TEST
1. Open Insights — with your report history you should see real patterns. Do they ring true? Anything feel like a stretch? (That would be a bug in our thresholds — report it.)
2. Check the Weekly Digest mentions an insight or two.
