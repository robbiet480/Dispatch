Build 24 (version 1.0)

NEW IN 24
- App Lock now keeps report content out of Spotlight by default. If you want locked reports to remain searchable, turn on "Spotlight Search While Locked" in Settings > Privacy.

IMPROVED
- Home has a calmer, more intentional layout: chart blocks and controls share consistent 20pt edges, the top controls leave more room for the data, and the bottom strip sits naturally around the home indicator.
- Repeated notification replans no longer spam the same nag-tail budget message in the logs.

WHAT TO TEST
1. Home: confirm the chart blocks, top controls, filter row, and bottom strip line up cleanly in light and dark across several questions. Check that labels and edge data points are not clipped.
2. With App Lock enabled, verify old and new reports do not appear in Spotlight by default. Turn on Settings > Privacy > Spotlight Search While Locked and confirm existing reports become searchable; turn it back off and confirm they disappear.
3. Check the bottom strip on a device with a home indicator: the dots and AWAKE control should remain easy to use and clear of the gesture area.
